Maghsal

The complex has a small but well planned and well-equipped facility for performing Ghusal of remains of deceased Muslims. The facility has marble floors and counters, hot and cold water connection, a shower room for volunteers for the service, an air conditioned room for keeping the remains, if needed, until they are transported to their burial places, and a room for storing supplies Lists of male and female volunteers are maintained to perform these services. The facility is used regularly by Shia and Sunni communities
